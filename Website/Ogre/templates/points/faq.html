FAQ

Q: What is OGRE?

Online Games and Resource Environment (OGRE) is an application used to motivate students to participate in University work. It does this by rewarding them with 'OGRE' points. To register you must have an account on the University of Glasgow's Moodle.

Q: How do I earn OGRE points?

Currently you can earn points by submitting assignments and completing quizzes on the University Moodle. We hope to later implement a system that rewards you for points for attending University lectures and labs.

Q: What can I spend OGRE points on?

You can spend your OGRE points on customising your avatar and playing games.

Q: What do I do if I forgot my password?

If you have forgotten your password please navigate to the login page and click the "Forgotten Password" hyperlink that will navigate you to the Moodle servers reset password screen. Once you have changed your password you MUST register your account again. Don't worry as you will not lose your existing OGRE points!

Q: What do I do if I forgot my username?

Please contact the development team using the contact page.
The staff will reply as soon as possible to help you to reset your username.

Q: How can I see my OGRE points?

You can see how many point you have on the dashboard and on your profile page. Additionally you can see how many points other users have on the leaderboard page, adding a competitive element to collecting OGRE points.

Q: Can I see a history of when I have earned/spent points?

Yes, you can. The feature "Points Transaction List" on the Dashboard will redirect you to a page which displays details of every OGRE point transaction you have ever made.
