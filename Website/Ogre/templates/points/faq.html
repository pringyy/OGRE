FAQ

Q: What is OGRE?

OGRE is an organisation that was created to encourage students to go to all their lectures, tutorials and labs.

Q: What can I spend OGRE points on?

You can spend your OGRE points on customising your avatar.

Q: How do I earn OGRE points?

By going to lectures, labs and tutorials and getting your cards scanned.

Q: What can I do if I lost my password?

In the login page, press the hyperlink named "Forgotten password" and you will be redirected to the moodle page for resetting password.

Notice: After you reset your password, you need to register your OGRE account again

Q: What can I do if I forget my username?

You should contact our staff by using the contact page.
The staff will then help you to reset the username.

Q: How can I see my OGRE points?

Your OGRE points is located in the profile page. When you click in to the profile page, press the column "OGRE points" and it will show you the details of your ORGE points

Q: Can I know where my OGRE points go?

Yes, you can. The feature named "Points Transaction List" in index page will redirect you to a page with a list of OGRE points, which show you the details of every OGRE point transaction
